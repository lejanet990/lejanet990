V O L U N T E E R

Being a volunteer is great! At the NWA Food Bank, there are numerous shifts available throughout the week. Volunteers can...

Work at a Warehouse Session

This is involves labeling, sorting and boxing foods for the mobile pantry. It's not uncommon for a group of people to build over 200 boxes of food in two hours, especially with the newly added conveyor belt that speeds the boxes along!

Work in the Teaching Garden

You never know what the Teaching Garden will bring! Tasks could include mulching, adding compost, weeding, planting, or harvesting!!

Help at a Mobile Pantry

Mobile pantries happen once a month in multiple locations! Volunteers assist with loading boxes of food into cars.

Other Ways Volunteers Help

Some other opportunities to help include submitting recipes to the Cutting Board, a collection of easy to make and nutritious meals. Other volunteer opportuntities include being a "middle mile" volunteer and driver. It's always a great idea to reach out and ask if you're interested!
